Adoption / fostering Procedures

To adopt / foster a baby or a young child from the Eromosele Orphanage Home is not impossible. It is not without labour! It takes nine months for a woman to bring a baby into the world. So adopters should be ready to go through the necessary procedures or fulfill the necessary conditions to adopt a baby. The first thing we look for in our adopters is love. Compassion must be visible. Adoption is an act of love. It must come from a desire to love a child. It must be motivated by our love for God. It must be because we want to raise a child for God as a demonstration of our love for God. Every child is a gift from God. Every child is an object of our love for God and for humanity. So if you want to adopt a child from the Eromosele Orphanage Home then be ready to love the child for the rest of your days. Be ready to be accountable for the success of that child. Be ready to be a good steward of the gift. Every child is a gift to humanity and if nourished properly will enhance the human race.

FIRST STEP

Visit the Adoption section of the Ministry of Youth Sports and Social Development, (block 18). Their office is in te Complex of the Government Offices located in Alausa, Ikeja, Lagos. They will put you through the series of interviews, a letter of authorization to adopt will be given to you. This letter is your key to joy and the patter of tiny feet! With this letter in your hand you can visit us at our office located in Palm grove Estate, Lagos

Please consider the following conditions before you come to us:

WE DO NOT CHARGE MONEY FOR ADOPTION, therefore don't offer money to any individual.
WE DO NOT PROCESS ADOPTION LETTER WITHOUT SIGHTING THE ORIGINAL LETTER OF APPROVAL.
WE CARRY OUT MEDICAL TEST FOR ALL CHILDREN.
WE LOVE TO RECEIVE REGULAR REPORTS OF CHILDREN ADOPTED.
WE DO NOT VISIT HOMES OF ADOPTERS AFTER ADOPTION,THIS IS THE RESPONSIBILITY OF THE SOCIAL WELFARE, therefore do not encourage orphanage staff to visit your homes.
MONITORING OF ADOPTED CHILDREN IS DONE BY THE SOCIAL WELFARE OF LAGOS STATE.
